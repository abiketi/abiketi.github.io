Hélios Solar System

A unique system, producing 100% green energy :
electricity, heat and cold

Hélios is a solar concentrated system using an innovative turbogenerator thermodynamic machine as energy converter. This patented machine operates according to the Intercooled Recuperative Reheat Gas Turbine cycle. This silent system requires no maintenance and offers high thermal efficiency. The power density is increased by twice compared to other turbogenerators which results in half mass flow rate. Hélios provides hot and cold thermal energetic power needs in addition to electric energetic need. Our vision behind the development of Hélios system is to democratize the solar energy by breaking cost and complexity barriers as well as to Reduce solar concentrator system cost, mitigate the photovoltaic environmental impacts and the geopolitical dependence on raw materiels

    RANKINE CYCLE

    Known also as vapor cycle, Rankine machines
    uses changing phase working fluid as
    working medium to achieve the Rankine
    thermodynamic cycle. This cycle uses a pump,
    a boiler (evaporator), an expander and a condenser.
    Water is used fot medium and high temperature
    sources and organic fluids for low temperature.
    We have designed different Rankine systems
    for low, medium and high temperature. Different
    expansion machines have been developed including:
    piston expander, turbocompressors,
    scroll and impulse turbines.

    SPLIT CYCLE ENGINE

    Split Cycle Engines are high efficiency
    Internal Combustion Engine where intake
    and compression phases are separated
    from the combustion/expansion and exhaust
    phases.
    This energy converters offers different
    advantages compared to conventional ICE:
    -Thermal management can offer higher
    performance and efficiency
    -Miller cycle can be achieved by simply
    increasing the length of the power cylinder.
    -Piston friction can be reduced by offsetting
    the compression and power cylinders
    -Design flexibility of the engine
    -Compatibility with additional devices
    like turbocharger and waste heat recovery
    -Higher thermal efficiencies can be achieved
    by using an internal regenerator
    We design an develop these Engines with
    power ranging from 1kW to 100 kW
    We design all components and we define
    the integration validation plan during
    the development phase and we perform
    characterization and endurance tests
